What We Do

Hi there! We’re Triple S- 7 Israeli and Palestinian students who have been studying computer science and entrepreneurship at a program called MEET for 3 years now. We’ve come to the conclusion that one of the biggest issues we all face today is stereotypes- whether based on gender, race, religion or appearance.

So, we decided to create a website where other open-minded people can share children’s stories (fiction/nonfiction) that show stereotypes being broken and read other shared stories. In order to make sure the content is kid-friendly and non-offensive we ask that you send us any stories you want to publish, to the email mentioned below Email: projecttriples@gmail.com.
