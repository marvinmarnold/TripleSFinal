About

Hello there! We are a group of Palestinian and Israeli teenagers in a program called MEET. We have been studying computer science and business management for 3 years now, and we've decided that it's finally time for us to create our own project.

So after many debates and a lot of Toffi, we have decided to devote our project to stereotypes and most importantly-BREAKING them! So we invite you to share with us your OWN stories (either real or made up) that take us a step closer to our vision- A world where society defies social expectations.
